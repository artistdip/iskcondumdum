Our Products

Pellets

Pelletizing is a method of agglomeration, or particle size enlargement, in which material fines are processed into pellets or granules. Pelletizing is used throughout a multitude of industries to process thousands of materials from difficult to handle powders and fines, into easy-to-handle pellets.

With many benefits, and a highly customizable process, pelletizing has become a staple in many industries, with new applications for this valuable process constantly on the rise.

Located at Rourkela, Odisha. The annual production capacity of the plant is 6,00,000 TPA. The Pellet Plant is backed with Beneficiation Plant to utilize the low-grade iron ore fines and convert them into a value-added product. It produces both blast furnace and DRI grade pellets. It is one of the fastest-growing companies in India catering to a vast and quality-focused customer base. It needs around milliontons of iron ore fines annually to meet its production target. The Utilization of the same is 90-95 %.

Billets

MS Billets are semi-finished casting product produced in a steel mill that needs further processing to transform it into finished good. It is a round or square cross-sectioned metal length which is created directly by the process of continuous casting. Billets are used to produce bar stock and wire, pipes or other cross-sectional structures Continuous Casting: Molten Metals are solidified by the process of continuous casting. It is a lower-cost production of metals with better quality. This process is used most frequently to cast steel, We at Shri Mahavir Ferro Alloys product 3 sizes of Mild Steel Billets
• 100 X 100
• 125 X 125
• 150 X 150

Some of the essential features of our product are:
 • They are Dimensionally correct and have Tremendous tensile strength
• These billets are High temperature tolerant and can withstand Extreme pressure conditions
• At the end of the process, casted billets are send to the laboratory for a stringent checking of their properties and forwarded to the Quality Control Department, which ensures they comply with IS: 2830 & 2831.
• The quality of the billets produced are free from any internal defects.
• These billets are produced in the steel melting shop at Shri Mahavir. The SMS facility has a capacity of 1,18,000 Tonnes annual. SMS also has 3 induction furnaces each of 12 tons in capacity along with a Continuous Casting machine of radius 6/11 and 2 Strands. They are supplied with Sponge Iron, Cast Iron and Scrap as raw materials to produce billets

Sponge Iron

• The plant has (3 X 100) TPD DRI Plant/Kiln with an installed capacity of (0.1 MTPA), (3 x 12)-ton and (1 x15) Induction Furnaces with an installed capacity of 0.10 MTPA billet and 12 MW Captive Power Plant. The Plant has also installeda 1MW Solar Power Plant. The plant has also its own railway siding, this is a private freight terminal. It is a spongy mass of iron which is a metallic product produced through direct reduction of iron ore/ iron pellet in the solid state. It is mainly used in making steel through the secondary route. The process of sponge iron making aims to remove the oxygen from iron ore or pellets.
• The Coal used at the plant are of 2 types
• Imported Coal – The plant uses the RB2 South African Coal.
• Domestic Coal – They plant uses MCL and SECL Coal for its production
• The department is planning to expand and has set the target to start a (1 x 350) and (1x 600) TPD DRI Plant/Kiln in the upcoming years.
• The major consumption of sponge iron is in-home to produce billets at the SMS area of the plant
• We sell sponge iron, as an intermediate product, primarily in the states of Odisha, Maharashtra , Bihar, Jharkhand ,Chhattisgarh etc.
