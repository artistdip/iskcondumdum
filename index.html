About Us

SMFAL is a world of enterprise and innovation inspired by its Founders Sri S.C.Jain . What now stands before the world is a leading, fully integrated producer of ferro alloys Stradling the value chain from manufacturing to smelting and with plans for accelerated growth.

SMFA is a private limited company incorporated on 10th September 1996 under The Companies Act, 1956 having production facilities of sponge iron, billets, pellets and captive power plant at Kalunga Industrial Estate in Odisha along with a Private Freight Terminal to facilitate transportation of materials. It has also commissioned a 1 MW solar power plant at Sundargarh, Odisha.
